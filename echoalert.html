Emergency Protocols



Critical information for emergency situations

In Case of Fire Emergency

If you discover a fire:

  1. Activate the nearest fire alarm
  2. Call the fire department immediately at 101
  3. Evacuate the building using the nearest safe exit
  4. Do not use elevators
  5. If trapped, seal doors with wet cloth and signal from window

Remember: Stop, Drop, and Roll if your clothes catch fire.

In Case of Medical Emergency

For medical emergencies:

  1. Call ambulance at 102 immediately
  2. Check if the person is responsive
  3. Begin CPR if trained and if person isn't breathing
  4. Use AED if available and follow voice prompts
  5. Stay with the person until help arrives

Important: Don't move injured person unless in immediate danger.
